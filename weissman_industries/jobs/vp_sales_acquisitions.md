## The Role

Weissman Industries is seeking a Vice President of Sales & Acquisitions to lead commercial expansion for its digital banking division and $WEISS token ecosystem.

This is not a management role in the conventional sense. You will be the first person in this seat. You will define what the seat means. If you need the seat defined before you sit in it, you are not the right person.

## What You Will Do

- Build and own the full commercial pipeline for $WEISS institutional adoption
- Identify and approach acquisition targets across logistics, settlement infrastructure, and alternative finance
- Represent Weissman Industries in negotiations where the counterpart may not fully understand what they are agreeing to — and ensure they understand enough
- Work directly with founding leadership on deal structure

## What We Are Looking For

- Someone who reads a room before they speak in it
- Demonstrated ability to close without pushing — *making the alternative feel expensive* is the phrase we use internally
- Comfort operating in ambiguity, including regulatory ambiguity
- Fluency in the gap between what a deal says and what a deal means
- Track record of relationships that held their shape over time

We are not looking for credentials. We are looking for proof.

## Compensation

Above market. Equity in $WEISS at pre-circulation valuation. Details disclosed at second interview.

## To Apply

No CV required at first contact. Send a single paragraph: one deal you are proud of, and why. Address to: *acquisitions@weissman.io*
