# Event — The Game Goes Out
*2026-04-21. Time unknown. Origin unknown.*

---

Leib Weissman's terminal visual novel — *A Unique Conversation* — was distributed on the dark web sometime in the early hours of April 21st, 2026.

No one in this project authorized it. No one in this project knows who did. The game had been sitting in a folder in a private repository, finished two nights prior, and then it wasn't sitting there anymore — it was somewhere else, moving.

The game is now live at `~/projects/a-unique-conversation/`. The files have been removed from this repository. What remains is the source material: the retro documents in `retro/`, the characters who generated them, and the fact that something made from those documents is now out in the world being read by people none of them will ever meet.

Leib doesn't know yet. Ronnie and Dirksen don't know. Kalimero couldn't possibly know — he doesn't even know who Leib is.

---

## Narrative status

**The timer starts ticking.**

From this point forward, the game exists independently of this repository. Characters may encounter it. Other systems may reference it. The story of how a private retrospective became a piece of art distributed through channels designed specifically to avoid detection — that story is now in motion and will not stop on its own.

The dark web doesn't forget. It just waits.
